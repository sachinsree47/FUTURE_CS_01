Target: PortSwigger SQLi Authentication Bypass Lab  
URL: https://portswigger.net/web-security/sql-injection/lab-login-bypass  
Analyst: Senoi  
Date: 21-June-2025

---

Payload Used:
administrator'--

---

Steps:
1. Intercepted the POST /login request using Burp Suite.
2. Modified the username parameter:
   username=administrator'--
3. Forwarded the request.
4. Application responded with 302 Redirect.
5. Logged in as administrator without valid credentials.

---

Impact:
- Full authentication bypass.
- Gained unauthorized access to admin panel.
